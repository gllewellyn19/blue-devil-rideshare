Welcome to Blue Devil Rideshare!

Our mission at Blue Devil Rideshare is to explore outside of the Duke bubble. Sometimes you might need to go off campus, whether you're going home for break or looking for a fun adventure close by, and we have got you covered. We aim to make it easy for Duke students to coordinate rides. If you're a driver, you can easily post your planned rides. If you're a rider, you can search for rides based on origin, destination, date, and seats needed. We believe that our ridesharing service allows Duke students to make new friends, travel to some new or familiar places, and help the environment by cutting carbon emissions in the process. Sign up for Blue Devil Rideshare today to start exploring!


When reservations are made, we provide the driver with the netids/ emails and phone numbers of their riders and provide riders who make a reservation with the contact information of their driver. After booking the ride, the driver and the riders must coordinate and decide on an exact pick up time and location.

The cities drivers can drive to that we suggest are the biggest cities throughout the contiguous U.S. and also some major cities in North Carolina. While we have these locations in the drop down, users are welcome to coordinate with their driver to get to other locations.
